Elite 7 Active

Overview

Ultra-compact design and active fit, built to withstand even the toughest workouts.

ELITE 7 ACTIVE

Free your workout

When you’re working up a sweat, you want to know your buds are going to stay firmly in place.

We’ve re-engineered our sports earbuds for the ultimate active fit with our unique ShakeGrip™ technology, designed to stay in. No matter how hard you work out.

Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) keeps you immersed in the music. The MySound feature in the Jabra Sound+ app personalizes your sound to your hearing. And with up to 8 hours listening time sets you free for even the longest workouts. We’ve pushed the boundaries to help you push yours.

SHAKEGRIP™ SECURE FIT

The only thing that moves is you

Whether you’re walking, running or repping, Elite 7 Active stay comfortably in place, thanks to our unique ShakeGrip™ technology.

Made with a special Liquid Silicone Rubber compound that holds the buds more securely in the ear without the need for ear wings, ShakeGrip™ is an ingenious material that feels incredibly soft despite its firm grip. Perfect for long periods of active use.

For the ultimate grip, no matter how tough your workout.

ACTIVE NOISE-CANCELLATION

Bring the sweat. Not the noise.

Nothing should intrude when you’re in the zone. That’s why powerful Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) kicks in with a simple tap of the button. Distracting background noise is blocked instantly, with adjustable levels of cancellation that can be set to your own preference using the Jabra Sound+ app.

Lose yourself in the music – and your workout.

HEARTHROUGH

Let the world in. Only when you want.

Immersion is great for focus, but sometimes you need to be aware of your surroundings. That’s where our HearThrough technology helps. Tap the button, and it uses the microphones in the earbuds to relay sounds around you.

And because different environments call for different levels of awareness, you can choose from five levels of HearThrough in the Jabra Sound+ app, to suit you. So if you’re out on a run, you can stay aware when crossing the road, or hear traffic approaching from behind.

Tap into the real world with HearThrough.
